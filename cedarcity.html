The Location

  • Cedar City: small city (pop. 35,000) with a vibrant art scene and known as an outdoor recreation basecamp
  • 2.5 hours to downtown Las Vegas
  • 4.5 hours to downtown Salt Lake City
  • High alpine forest and desert with warm summers and snowy winters

  • Endless local rockclimbing, hiking, and backcountry skiing.
  • Zion, Bryce Canyon, and the Grand Canyon in daytrip range
