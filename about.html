About Us

Origin Story

This project started as an attempt to answer the question: “What would the ideal community look like?”

Exiting college, we were disillusioned with the lack of meaningful connection found in traditional housing models. We couldn't find anyone in our network truly satisfied with the design of their community, whether urban, suburban, or rural.

Inspired by the rising success coliving movements and the exponentially growing investment in the space, we set out to design a flexible and scalable blueprint for coliving communities.

Grassroots coliving houses are decentralized — none have figured out how to scale and interconnect with other community pods while retaining the strength and flexibility of their individual personality.

Large-scale coliving companies simply lease and rebrand multi-family buildings — this increases profit margins, but does little to actually foster community.

Cosm is engineering the full coliving stack:

  • Directly purchasing property: no money lost through middling leases → lower rent
  • Designing the built environment specifically for coliving and coworking → intentional design, not repurposed apartments
  • Developing scalable network-selection processes → enable communities to remain at full occupany while also retaining their unique culture and values
  • Providing flexible ammenities and community management services → removing the logistic difficulties behind renting
  • Creating a network effect between coliving locations → connect locally and travel globally

 

We truly believe that this model of living can help us become happier, more connected, and more innovative. The future is bright, see you there :)
